# TERM SHEET

## TROPTIONS, INC. and VEX Technologies, LLC

**Project:** VEX (\$VEX) Launchpad & Exchange Integration  
**Date:** May 2026  
**This Term Sheet is non-binding except as to confidentiality.**

---

### 1. PARTIES

- **TROPTIONS, INC.** ("TROPTIONS"), a Wyoming corporation, operator of the UNYKORN tokenization infrastructure and TROPTIONS Exchange OS.
- **VEX Technologies, LLC** ("VEX"), a Wyoming limited liability company, developer of the VEX (\$VEX) protocol.

### 2. TRANSACTION OVERVIEW

TROPTIONS shall serve as the **exclusive initial launchpad and preferred exchange infrastructure partner** for the \$VEX SPL token on Solana. VEX shall integrate \$VEX with the TROPTIONS Exchange OS and merchant/partner ecosystem.

### 3. ECONOMIC TERMS

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Platform Integration Fee** | **VEX shall pay TROPTIONS $10,000** within five (5) business days of execution of definitive agreements (after Section 5 gates). **Non-refundable.** Covers TROPTIONS technical integration, liquidity pool setup, Exchange OS listing, and initial co-marketing per Schedule E. **VEX funds its own audit, Wyoming filings, and issuer marketing.** |
| **MOU superseded** | MOU §1.02 is **rejected**: no TROPTIONS "investment," no guaranteed reimbursement from project funds, marketing budget, Day-1 revenue, or fee revenue. |
| **Optional treasury buy** | TROPTIONS may, at its sole discretion, purchase \$VEX on the open market post–Public Sale for inventory — **at risk of total loss**, with public disclosure within 24 hours. Not required for listing. |
| **Fee Share** | **Tiered structure:** Months 1–6: **60% TROPTIONS / 40% VEX**; Months 7–12: **50% TROPTIONS / 50% VEX**; Month 13+: **40% TROPTIONS / 60% VEX**. |
| **Definition of Net Fees** | Gross trading/launchpad fees attributable to \$VEX activity on TROPTIONS-operated platforms, less on-chain transaction costs, chargebacks, and taxes. **No deduction for overhead or salaries.** |
| **Payment** | Monthly, within 15 business days after month-end, with on-chain data export and right of independent blockchain verification. |

### 3A. SMART CONTRACT OWNERSHIP (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

- All \$VEX token programs, oracle hooks, NFT collateral, and on-chain SEO/R2O enforcement shall be **deployed and controlled by TROPTIONS** (audited `rwa-realestate` stack + `launch.unykorn.org`).
- TROPTIONS will **not** list unaudited contracts authored by VEX or affiliates. White Paper code snippets are not production deliverables.
- **VEX retains** off-chain SEO/R2O legal templates and marketing IP, subject to executed IP Assignment for any future on-chain contributions.
- **VEX bears** Tier-1 audit costs for net-new modules (up to **$15,000** cap) — separate from the Integration Fee.
- Future VEX DAO governance **cannot** override TROPTIONS kill switch, fee split, or contract ownership.

### 4. EXCLUSIVITY

- **Scope:** TROPTIONS is VEX's exclusive Solana launchpad for the **initial 6 months** from Public Sale Date (TROPTIONS-operated launchpad and liquidity pools only).
- **Carve-Out:** VEX may maintain listings on centralized exchanges (BitMart, Poloniex, etc.) and non-TROPTIONS DEX pools without restriction.
- **Performance:** If TROPTIONS fails to generate **$100,000** in VEX-attributed trading volume and **500 unique wallets** in the first **90 days** after Public Sale, exclusivity **suspends** (VEX may designate another Solana launchpad after 30 days' notice).
- **Right of First Refusal:** After Month 6, TROPTIONS has ROFR on any Additional Token launches by VEX on terms no less favorable than any third party.

### 5. CONDITIONS PRECEDENT (GATES TO CLOSE)

No integration obligations (and no obligation for TROPTIONS to pay VEX any funds) shall arise until VEX delivers and TROPTIONS accepts:

1. Independent securities counsel opinion that \$VEX is not an investment contract under *Howey* (counsel independent of issuer principals);
2. Reconciliation of the Operating Agreement securities disclaimer with the consumptive-utility characterization, evidenced by amendment or side letter;
3. Wyoming Notice of Intent filing confirmation (W.S. § 34-29-106);
4. Tier-1 smart contract audit (OtterSec, Trail of Bits, CertiK, or equivalent) with zero unresolved Critical/High findings;
5. SPL token mint address, program IDs, and treasury multisig disclosure (mint renounced or ≥3-of-N multisig with at least one TROPTIONS-observable key);
6. **$25,000 USDC** deposited into TROPTIONS-designated legal defense escrow;
7. TROPTIONS confirmation of FinCEN MSB / Wyoming money-transmitter compliance status.

### 6. REGULATORY PROTECTIONS

- **Kill Switch:** TROPTIONS may immediately suspend trading, freeze liquidity, and withhold payments upon any SEC, state, or FinCEN enforcement action, Wells notice, or upon written advice of counsel that continued activity creates material regulatory risk. **No liability for permitted suspension.**
- **Indemnification:** VEX shall indemnify TROPTIONS for claims arising from the token being deemed an unregistered security or from VEX's real estate (SEO/R2O) activities, subject to definitive agreement limitations.

### 7. MARKETING (VEX)

Quarterly minimum: four (4) co-branded social posts, one (1) joint AMA, footer/logo placement on VEX.io, and exchange announcements only where CEX listing agreements permit. No open-ended "good faith volume" obligation.

### 8. DEFINITIVE AGREEMENTS

This term sheet is non-binding except for confidentiality. The parties shall negotiate in good faith toward a definitive Master Platform Services Agreement incorporating these terms within **14 days** of this term sheet's acceptance.
