**Assignor:** VEX Technologies, LLC and affiliates (including Judson Charles, Inc., EVEX Management, LLC, EVEX Holdings, LLC, and any future "VEX DAO, LLC") ("Assignor")

**Assignee:** TROPTIONS, INC. ("Assignee")

**Effective Date:** _______________

---

## 1. ASSIGNMENT

Assignor hereby assigns to Assignee all right, title, and interest in and to:

(a) All smart contract source code, program IDs, bytecode, deployment scripts, and upgrade keys relating to the \$VEX token, SEO/R2O settlement logic, oracles, and on-chain enforcement;

(b) All technical documentation, API specifications, and integration materials describing on-chain behavior of the \$VEX protocol;

(c) All inventions, discoveries, and improvements conceived in connection with the Master Platform Agreement that relate to **on-chain** implementation.

**Excluded (Assignor retains):** Off-chain SEO/R2O legal agreements, state compliance memos, marketing materials, trademarks, and business methods that do not include executable code.

---

## 2. REPRESENTATIONS

Assignor represents that, to Assignor's knowledge, no third party owns conflicting rights to assigned materials, and Assignor has not deployed production \$VEX contracts as of the Effective Date.

Assignor shall not deploy, or permit deployment of, \$VEX-related smart contracts without Assignee's prior written consent after the Effective Date.

---

## 3. FUTURE CODE

Any smart contract or technical infrastructure developed by either party under the Master Agreement shall be owned by **Assignee**, with Assignor receiving only a limited license to reference program IDs and public interfaces for legal and marketing purposes.

---

## 4. CONSIDERATION

Assignment is made in connection with the Master Platform Agreement and Platform Integration Fee / revenue share. Separate consideration: **$1.00** and other good and valuable consideration.

---

## 5. DAO ACKNOWLEDGMENT

If Assignor forms VEX DAO, LLC or transfers governance tokens, such entity shall be bound by this Assignment and shall not revoke Assignee's ownership of deployed infrastructure or override Assignee's kill switch or fee mechanics without Assignee's written consent.
