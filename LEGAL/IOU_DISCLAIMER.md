# TROPTIONS IOU Disclosure

## What TROPTIONS Issues

TROPTIONS, Inc. issues **IOUs (trust-line tokens)** on the XRP Ledger and Stellar Network. These are gateway receipts, not native stablecoins issued by Circle, Tether, or MakerDAO.

| IOU | Represented Asset | Backing Claim |
|-----|------------------|---------------|
| TROPTIONS | Platform trade currency | TROPTIONS platform asset reserve |
| USDC IOU | USD Coin (Circle) | Claim on USDC held in gateway custody |
| USDT IOU | Tether USD | Claim on USDT held in gateway custody |
| DAI IOU | Dai (MakerDAO/Sky) | Claim on DAI held in gateway custody |
| EURC IOU | Euro Coin (Circle) | Claim on EURC held in gateway custody |

## Reserve Position

The TROPTIONS Gateway asserts that USDC IOUs are backed by a documented **$175 million USDC trade desk position** (Bryan Stone USDC 175M, Chainlink-validated, CIS package). Redemption of IOUs for underlying assets is governed by the TROPTIONS Gateway Terms of Service and requires KYC/AML verification.

## Important

- TROPTIONS IOUs are **not FDIC insured**.
- They are **not bank deposits**.
- Value may fluctuate based on reserve verification and market liquidity.
- Participation is through direct engagement with the institutional platform.
